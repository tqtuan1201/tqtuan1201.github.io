Phrases Define More
No wonder (I am) not surprised at all (that). To express a lack of surprise or understanding about something. It is often used to indicate that a particular situation or outcome is not unexpected, based on the information or context that is known - No wonder she won the race - she’s been training for months!
- No wonder you’re exhausted - you’ve been working non-stop for days!
